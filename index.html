Jolly Phonics

Transforming children's ability

As the leading synthetic phonics publisher, and the most experienced, we offer a 7-year programme that teaches not only phonics, but spelling, punctuation and grammar too, enabling children to read and write from an early age.

The Jolly Phonics Handbook (in print letters) is a complete resource for teaching young children the skills they need to read and write fluently in their first year of school. This book is a comprehensive resource for teachers who want the flexibility of teaching the program with worksheets and other photocopiable resources in the classroom. It offers step-by-step guidance on all aspects of the teaching across the year, as well as a wealth of practical ideas and fun activities for the children.
